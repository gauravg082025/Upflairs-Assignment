My Travel Bucket List

Exploring the world is one of my biggest dreams! Here are the top three destinations I hope to visit in my lifetime.

  1. Kyoto, Japan

    Kyoto is famous for its ancient temples, traditional wooden houses, beautiful gardens, and seasonal cherry blossoms. It's a place where history and nature blend seamlessly.

    Learn more about Kyoto

  2. Reykjavík, Iceland

    Known for its stunning natural landscapes, Reykjavík is the perfect base for seeing the Northern Lights, geysers, volcanoes, and majestic waterfalls.

    Explore Reykjavík

  3. Venice, Italy

    Venice is a magical city built on water, full of romance, art, and history. From gondola rides to Renaissance architecture, it offers a unique cultural experience.

    Discover Venice
